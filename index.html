Guess The Number

Rules:

  • The system will generate a random 5-digit number.
  • You must guess the number by entering exactly 5 digits.
  • Each guess must be a 5-digit number (no more, no less).
  • After each guess, you will be told how many digits are in the correct position.
  • The game continues until all 5 digits are in the correct position.
  • Your total number of guesses will be counted.
  • Once you guess correctly, you can reset the game to play again.
